CFDs

WHAT ARE CFDs

CFD (Contract for Difference) trading is an arrangement in financial derivatives trading stipulating the buyer to pay the seller the difference between the current value of the bought asset and the value at the time the contract was created. CFDs are used by investors to speculate on the price of the underlying asset, whether it will rise or fall.

CFDs have no expiration date and there is no delivery of physical goods involved, as you never own an actual product.
