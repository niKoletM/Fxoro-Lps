FXORO Products

  • Forex

    • The Forex is the largest and most widely used financial market by far, hosting a daily trading volume of over £ 4 billion. It is highly appreciated by traders who wish to trade on the values of currency pairs. Forex, in fact, comes from the terms Foreign Exchange Market, or the global market where currency trading takes place. The growing interest in Forex is certainly linked to the volatility of currencies. With FXORO it is possible to operate on Forex with the main world currencies, having access to more than 60 currency pairs, 24 hours a day and 5 days a week, through the three classic financial instruments: Spot Market, Forward Market and Futures Market.

  • Shares

    • FXORO offers the possibility to trade CFDs online with shares. CFDs are known as Contract For Difference, or Contracts for Difference. These are derivative instruments that allow the trader to operate on a specific financial market without buying or selling the underlying asset. Ultimately, through CFDs, you risk on the possible increase or decrease in a financial market, such as a share , or an index, a commodity, a cryptocurrency, an ETF or a currency. It is therefore a negotiation on the price difference. With FXORO it is possible to trade CFDs on shares gaining immediate access to the main global markets, among which the United States, the United Kingdom, Asia and Europe stand out.

  • Commodities

    • Trading on commodities is undoubtedly among the investment strategies most used by traders. Among the most popular commodities for this type of trading are gold, silver and oil. With FXORO it is however possible to trade also on Copper, Oil, Brent, Ngas, Coffee, Sugar, Corn, Wheat, Sbeam, Cotton and Cocoa. Commodity Trading (or, precisely, trading on Commodities) is based on the negotiation of these assets in order to make a profit on their price fluctuations, which can be up or down. FXORO allows Commodity Trading through the main derivative instruments, such as Futures, CFDs and ETCs.

  • Indices

    • Trading on indices consists of financial transactions initiated with the aim of making a profit on changes in the prices of indices. Financial indices are synthetic indicators that group the financial performance of a given pool of securities, grouped according to a predefined criterion. Among the most famous we find the Dow Jones, the Nasdaq 100, the S&P 500, the Euro Stoxx 50, the FTSE 100, the DAX, the Nikkei 225 and the CAC40. With FXORO it is possible to trade on these indices and on others such as the SMI20, the Gaming and E-sports Giants and the ASX200.

  • ETF

    • The ETFs, which stands for Exchange Traded Funds, began trading in 1993. There are now more than 5,000 ETFs available for $ 3 trillion worth of assets. These are similar products to investment funds and essentially represent a fund invested on the stock exchange that faithfully replicates the performance of an index that invests in stocks, bonds or other assets. There are two types of ETFs, physically replicating and synthetically replicating ETFs. The former buy and replicate all the securities in an index (full replication) or only a part (partial replication). The latter, on the other hand, replicate the reference index through the signing of swap contracts with a counterparty, usually a bank.

  • CFDs on Cryptocurrencies

    • CFD trading on cryptocurrencies is very popular today, because it consists of speculating on the appreciation or depreciation of a crypto, without buying it directly. With FXORO it is possible to operate with the main cryptocurrencies on the market, among which Bitcoin, Litecoin, Ethereum, Dash and Dogecoin stand out.

How to open an account with FXORO

With FXORO it is possible to open trading accounts to invest in the financial markets. Our broker provides the possibility to open an account by choosing from the 4 main types available on the market: Fixed Spread Account, Variable Spread Account, ECN Account and Islamic Account. The Fixed Spread Account provides a spread, that is the difference between the purchase price and the sale price, fixed and always identical. On the contrary, the Variable Spread Account has a floating spread, which depends on the buy and sell prices at the time of the investment. The ECN account belongs to the category of No Dealing Desk. This means that in this case the broker does not act as the user's counterpart, but only as an intermediary for accessing the financial markets. Finally, you can open with FXORO an Islamic Account, or an account specially set up to respect Islamic law in terms of loans and finance, a discipline in which any collection of interest corresponds is illegal. For this reason, the Islamic Account is set up in such a way as to avoid the creation of Swap commissions and Overnight interests.
